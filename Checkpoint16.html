Checkpoint-16

Completed
Play Time: ~20mins

  • Check it out
Role: Producer, Developer/Programmer, and Writer
Engine: Twine (HTML)
Team Size: 2
Summary: After your partner goes missing, you explore Checkpoint-16, a galactic rest stop, in search of any information on their disappearance. However, time is running out. You only have a 24 hour leave before you're summoned back to HQ. Will you be able to find out what happened to your partner? Will you be able to save them? Interact and talk to the residents of Checkpoint-16, building rapport with certain characters to gain and unlock information.
Contributions: Story creation and implementation. Created a time mechanic along with tracking player choices during NPC interactions. Implemented all elements into Twine.
