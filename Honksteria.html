Honksteria
Global Game Jam 2024

Currently in Progress
Current demo includes player movement, toxic laughing gas, base level and map design, and 3D art assets.

  • Check it out
Role: Developer/Programmer
Engine: Unity
Team Size: 8
Summary: Players are introduced as a clown who has just recently gotten their egg, which symbolizes their achievement of registering as a clown. It is a tradition that began in 1946 which became a way for clowns to "copyright" their faces. Players are directed to the ringmaster's tent for this happy event when suddenly a plume of gas goes up from inside, and the ringmaster comes stumbling out maniacally giggling. The happy and bright carnival suddenly descends into chaos and maniacle laughter. Other clowns start targetting the player, and the player must stealthily and speedily make their way out of the carnival; the exit marked by a ferris wheel.
Contributions: Programmed player movements (shift-run, default-walk, left ctrl-crouch) and first person camera perspective. Attempted and worked on AI pathing and steering, more specifically obstacle avoidance. Assisted in NPC programming.
